1ยบ Step: Select options
  1. Start by selecting the desired quiz area.
  2. Once you've chosen the area, select the specific technology within that area.
  3. Adjust the difficulty level of the quiz based on your preference and knowledge of the selected area and technology.
  4. After making these selections, you can begin the quiz by clicking the "Get start" button.
2ยบ Step: Make the quiz
  1. The quiz consists of a total of 10 questions.
  2. Each question consists of a question with 5 alternatives, of which only one is correct.
  3. You have the flexibility to navigate back and forth between the 10 questions.
  4. Once you are confident with your answers for all 10 questions, you can choose the "Finish" in tenth question.
3ยบ Step: See the results
  1. When you finish th quiz, it will show you how many correct answers you had in total.
  2. There is a "Here" button to get more information about your quiz performance.
  3. By clicking on the button for more information, you will be able to navigate between the questions and check the questions, selected alternatives and correct alternatives.
